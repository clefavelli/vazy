Vazy Subscriptions

We have four subscriptions:

  • Bronze –KES 999
  • BSilver – KES 3000
  • Gold – KES 5000
  • Platinum – 10,000


For example for Bronze:

Subscription fee is KES 999. Subscriber can only borrow an outfit worth up to KES 999

Subscriber pays the store 20% their subscription to rent, wear and return. Eg if a dress is 800, a bronze subscriber qualifies to carry the dress and will pay KES 200 to the store.

So if a Dress goes for KES 1000 then the Renting Fee is KES 200
